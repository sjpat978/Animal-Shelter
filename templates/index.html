The Introduction

Animal Shelter Site is a free service for both customers and animal shelter staff!

Customers get access to a large database of adoptable animals in shelters near them, as well as a matching service to an animal that would be the best for them. Staff get access to a large database of their shelter's inventory (food, medication, animals, etc.), animal profiles, and prospective customers.

Features

Communicate

Customers can communicate with staff about any concerns or questions they have.

Up-to-Date

Staff can update and add new information to the adoption database.

Easy Access

Customers and staff have easy access to animal profiles through our database.

Personalization

Customers can personalize their search results via our matching service
